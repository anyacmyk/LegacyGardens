About Legacy Gardens

Legacy Gardens is a serene online space where you can create a personalized memorial to honor and celebrate the life of your loved one. With our interactive platform, you can design a virtual tribute that reflects their spirit, using custom photos, heartfelt messages, and beautiful elements like flowers, which you can simply drag and drop onto the memorial page.

At Legacy Gardens, we believe that memories deserve to be cherished in a way that fosters healing and reflection. Our core values of compassion, respect, and remembrance are at the heart of everything we do. We offer families a meaningful way to keep the legacy of their loved ones alive, creating a lasting digital garden where stories, emotions, and memories can bloom.

We are committed to providing a peaceful and supportive environment, where each memorial is unique, and where family and friends can visit and contribute anytime. At Legacy Gardens, we believe in the power of memories and the beauty of shared connections. Let us help you build a space of love, peace, and eternal remembrance.

Datasets

Photos 1914 - 1918: This data comes from the queensland goverment and contains images from 1914-1918. The solider images, names and descriptions have come from this dataset. Any additional data that the user creates will be stores locally. As there is a subject column in this dataset, the soldier portraits are filtered by this so only them can be shown.




Wildlife: This data comes from ALA or the Atlas of Living Australia. It has information about flora and fauna of Australia and the regions that they come from. Images provided from the dataset will also be used to show what the flower will look like.




API's

Map Box: This is an API that has information about continents, states and cities. This will be used to map the flowers into reigons.
